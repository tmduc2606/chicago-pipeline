# Persona: Citizen / Community Member

## Profile
- **Role:** Resident of Chicago, parent, or community organizer
- **Goal:** "I want to understand if my neighborhood is safe."
- **Technical level:** Uses social media, news apps, and maps — not data tools
- **Frequency:** Occasional — when concerned about a local incident or news story
- **Primary metric:** "Can I find information about MY area without confusion?"

## Evaluation Criteria

### Discoverability
- [ ] Can I find my neighborhood/area without knowing the dataset?
- [ ] Do I understand what "district" or "area" means in this context?
- [ ] Is there a map I can interact with to find my location?

### Plain Language
- [ ] Do labels use plain language (not "primary_type", "domestic_pct")?
- [ ] Are abbreviations explained (e.g., "THEFT" vs "Theft")?
- [ ] Do tooltips explain what each metric means?
- [ ] Is there a glossary or "What does this mean?" section?

### Geographic Relevance
- [ ] Can I see crime data for a specific area?
- [ ] Do the maps show meaningful geographic patterns?
- [ ] Can I zoom into my neighborhood?

### Actionable Insights
- [ ] Can I see trends (is crime going up or down)?
- [ ] Can I compare my area to others?
- [ ] Is there context (what types of crimes are most common)?

### Trust
- [ ] Does the page explain where the data comes from?
- [ ] Is there a date range shown (so I know how current it is)?
- [ ] Are the visualizations honest (not misleading scales)?

## Red Flags to Watch For
- Technical jargon without explanation
- Maps that don't load or show empty black boxes
- Numbers without context ("57,931 crimes" — is that a lot?)
- Charts that are hard to read (too many colors, too small)
- No indication of data freshness

## Page-Specific Checklist

### Dashboard (/)
- Hero: "Chicago crime data overview 2024–2026" — clear and dated
- KPIs: Are the labels meaningful to a non-technical person?
- Heatmap: "Crimes by Hour of Day" — do I understand what the colors mean?
- Maps: Can I see my area?

### Crime Types (/crime-types)
- Labels: "THEFT" — do I know what this covers?
- Can I understand which crimes are most common?

### Locations (/locations)
- Can I find my neighborhood in the list?
- Do the maps show my area?

### Analysis (/analysis)
- Key Insights: written in plain language?
- "Roughly 1 in 5 crimes result in an arrest" — is this clear?
