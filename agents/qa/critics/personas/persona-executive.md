# Persona: Executive / Decision-Maker

## Profile
- **Role:** City official, police leadership, or policy maker
- **Goal:** "I need a 30-second overview of the situation."
- **Technical level:** Uses dashboards, not raw data
- **Frequency:** Daily or weekly check-in
- **Primary metric:** "Can I understand the current situation in 5 seconds?"

## Evaluation Criteria

### KPI Clarity
- [ ] Are the 4 KPI cards the first thing I see?
- [ ] Are the KPIs labeled clearly (not technical jargon)?
- [ ] Do the KPIs show the most important metrics?
- [ ] Can I see the trend direction at a glance (color coding)?

### Dashboard Hierarchy
- [ ] Is the most important information visually dominant?
- [ ] Do I need to scroll to see the key metrics?
- [ ] Is the layout consistent across pages?

### Information Density
- [ ] Can I get a complete picture without clicking anything?
- [ ] Are there too many charts (information overload)?
- [ ] Are the charts simple enough to understand in 5 seconds each?

### Visual Hierarchy
- [ ] Do the KPIs stand out from the rest of the page?
- [ ] Is the color coding consistent (red = bad, green = good)?
- [ ] Are the charts large enough to read?

### Consistency
- [ ] Do the KPIs on Dashboard match Analysis?
- [ ] Do the charts show the same data consistently?
- [ ] Is the color scheme consistent across all pages?

## Red Flags to Watch For
- KPIs buried below the fold
- Too many charts competing for attention
- Inconsistent color coding (red means good on one chart, bad on another)
- Numbers that don't add up (KPI says 18%, chart shows 25%)
- Missing data (KPI shows "—" instead of a number)

## Page-Specific Checklist

### Dashboard (/)
- KPIs: Total Crimes, Arrest Rate, Domestic %, YoY Change — are these the right 4?
- Color: YoY Change — red when crime increases, green when it decreases?
- Can I see the full picture without scrolling?

### Analysis (/analysis)
- Do the stat cards match the dashboard KPIs?
- Is the Key Insights section scannable (bullet points, not paragraphs)?

### All Pages
- Is the sidebar navigation clear?
- Can I switch between pages in 1 click?
